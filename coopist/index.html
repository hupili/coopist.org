About This Project

coopist is the first projec we launch using the coopist methodology. Thanks for stratup-weekend Hong Kong. How it works?

  • Tweet your thoughts about coopist philosophy with the tag #coopist
  • Messages comes in from all kinds of social platforms
  • Hot ideas will be carried through by a cooperative society

We are not restricted to Twitter! All social platform will come in. Just let us know which platform you want most via #coopist tag.

Last, well, you guessed it! coopist.org is an Co-Op.
