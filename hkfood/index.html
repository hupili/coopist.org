About This Project

Do you notice that there is a big gap between retail price and wholesale price? Consumers coming into the shops alone do not get a good deal. Why not form a consumer-Co-Op? Why not buy one, instead of two, directly to get better price?

Sounds similar? Like Vendor Relationship Management ? Crowd ownership (we just created at #SWHK to differentiate from crowd funding)?
